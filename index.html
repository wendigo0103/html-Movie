Best movies according to Roshan Samuel

My top 3 movies of all-time

Taare Zameen Par

This amazing movie is about a kid, Ishaan who suffers from dyslexia and how his teacher made him realise his dreams and work through his weaknesses while improving in which he already excelled at


3 Idiots

Starring Amir Khan as the lead role, Racho. This movie gives us the glimpse of Challenges, struggles and Confusion that comes when deciding for a career in India that prioritizes very few proffessions. This movie is about 3 friends that got in a top tier college, found their PASSION and the beauty of doing something you love as a proffession and turning it into a successful career.


The Social Dilemma

Documentary in which the tech experts from silicon valley alarm us with the influence and control of Big Tech has on our lives and how manipulative they can be with their actions. Apps like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Youtube etc. consume a big chunk of time from a teenagers life which destroys their future and makes them easy to manipulate. This Documentary will open your eyes to this destructive system.
